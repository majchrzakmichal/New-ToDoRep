Personas

Profiles of final app users.

Rosaline Hanata

Rosaline Hanata has 2 kids and a dog. She has to feed everyone, walk the dog, wash her hair, taking care of the children and prepair herself for an important meetings. Rosaline is very worried that she might forget to do something the next day and be late for her meeting.

When Rosaline opens this app she will have to tap on the green plus button. After she does that, she will see a text box, that she needs to state in a task. After pressing the submit button, she'll repeat the same action, until she gets her to do plan done for the day. After she completes one of her chores, she'd press the bullet point next to what she wrote, and that will automatically check it off from the list.

Bart Robinson

Bart Robinson is a 65 year old retired dentist who lives alone with a cat. Bart likes to spoil pet with treats, but when he goes out shopping, he always forgets at least one thing. This really annoys him. This is why he needs ToDoApp.

When he opens the app, he can use it as a shopping list. All he has to do is to add all the items he needs by pressing the green button. Once it's in the cart, Bart can tick it of from the list.
