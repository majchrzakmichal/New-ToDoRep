Bring!

Shopping list application

The app is nicely designed, layout is quite simple. It has a search box at the top, home button in the left top corner and drop-down lists of ingredients. Thanks to clever design it eliminates any extra clicks and make it very easy to use. User can pick an item from drop-down menus or just search it in the search box. Once the item is bought it just take to click on it and it removes from the list. It also has recently used menu that allows you to store your favourite shopping items at your convenience. Application also has ability to share your shopping list with other users so your partner or a friend can update some missing items for you. Another unique function is the inspiration button. It will show you list of dishes and when you click on it displays a list of ingredients that are needed to cook that meal

Habitica

It is a unique to do application that in many ways look like a game. It allows you to create a character and by completing your tasks you gain experience. Once you have enough exp you level up! It has a lot o features and could be fun. The downside is the monthly subscription.
